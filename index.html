OUR STORY

The Black Ash was founded in 1996 by Margret and jonathan Ash, the ambitious farmer couple from Texas and early on what it meant to not only raise
cattle in a sustainable and animal-friendly way but also to appreciate the taste of an excellent meat dish. Together the move to California and change the
resturant culture with the opening of black ash for good. since then, guest have been experiencing what it means to eat high quality meat dishes in a rustic
and modern atmosphere.
